Model C503 – Curved Grand Auditorium Acoustic

The Model C503 is a refined interpretation of the classic Grand Auditorium, reimagined with a more pronounced waist and graceful curvature for both aesthetic elegance and tonal clarity. With a body length of 503 mm, a wide 394 mm lower bout, and a sculpted 208 mm waist, this guitar strikes a beautiful balance between power and precision. The tightened waist enhances midrange focus and player comfort, while the generous lower bout delivers a strong, open bass response. A slightly smaller upper bout improves articulation and ergonomics without sacrificing volume. Ideal for both fingerstyle and light strumming, the C503 offers a clear, balanced voice with excellent projection and a touch of warmth. The cutaway is in the Venetian style, although Florentine cutaways are also possible. Whether in the studio or on stage, this custom body shape delivers a responsive and expressive experience — blending modern versatility with a handcrafted, classical influence.

  • Soundboard – European Spruce
  • Body – Maple and Zimbabwean teak
  • Neck - Maple and Zimbabwean teak
  • Fretboard – Ebony
  • Bridge – Ebony
  • Scale length – 645mm
  • Includes a passive pickup system

Model C460 – Compact Concert Acoustic

A slim-waisted, small-bodied steel-string guitar inspired by the Orchestra Model but scaled down for comfort and responsiveness. With a body length of 460 mm and a lower bout of 340 mm, it offers articulate mids, clear trebles, and surprising warmth for its size. Ideal for fingerstyle players, songwriters, or anyone looking for a smaller and expressive instrument with elegant proportions.

  • Soundboard – Sitka Spruce
  • Body – Imbuia and Zimbabwean teak
  • Neck - Imbuia and Zimbabwean teak
  • Fretboard – Wenge
  • Bridge – Wenge
  • Scale length – 635mm
  • Nut – 44mm

Model C473 – Concert Classical Guitar

The Model C473 is a carefully crafted classical guitar with a body length of 473 mm, designed for tonal balance, elegant response, and a contemporary feel. Inspired by traditional Spanish designs but subtly reshaped for improved projection and clarity, it features a slightly smaller body and a full-depth profile to enhance warmth and volume. The soundboard is built with asymmetrical fan bracing, bringing the treble side subtly forward for better separation and brilliance in the upper registers. A lighter, more flexible bass side supports warmth and sustain without sacrificing clarity. This dynamic voicing results in a guitar that delivers rich bass with clean, expressive trebles — overcoming the common imbalance found in many classical guitars. The rosette, purfling, and custom detailing reflect a commitment to clean, handcrafted aesthetics. With a 52 mm nut, a 650 mm scale, and a carefully profiled neck, the C473 offers the tactile feel and articulation that classical players expect — whether on stage, in the studio, or for personal expression.

  • Body length: 473 mm
  • Upper bout: 277 mm
  • Waist: ~230–240 mm
  • Lower bout: ~360–370 mm
  • Depth: Upper bout ~95 mm, lower bout ~100–105 mm
  • Scale length: 650 mm
  • Nut width: 52 mm
  • Bracing: 7-fan asymmetrical
  • Includes a passive pickup system
